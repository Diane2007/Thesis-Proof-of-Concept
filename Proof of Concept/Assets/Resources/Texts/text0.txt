Grandma's Homecoming
Ethnic Communities Returning to their Ancestral Land
by Northern Journal

...when Alasha was seven, the former regime seized her family's land, driving Alasha and thousands of other Nakshatra people like her away from their ancestral soil. With the Leader's decree to returning land to native tribes, Alasha, now 97, returned to her childhood home after 90 years of war and displacement. Soldiers who assisted her tribe's homecoming were surprised to receive baskets of traditional Nakshatra pastry from the lady and her granddaughters..."
